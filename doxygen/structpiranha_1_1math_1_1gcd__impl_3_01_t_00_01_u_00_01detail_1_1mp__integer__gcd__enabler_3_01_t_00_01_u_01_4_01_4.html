Detailed Description

template<typename T, typename U>
struct piranha::math::gcd_impl< T, U, detail::mp_integer_gcd_enabler< T, U > >

Implementation of piranha::math::gcd() for piranha::mp_integer.

This specialisation is enabled when:

  • T and U are both instances of piranha::mp_integer,
  • T is an instance of piranha::mp_integer and U is an integral type,
  • U is an instance of piranha::mp_integer and T is an integral type.

The result will be calculated via piranha::mp_integer::gcd(), after any necessary type conversion.
