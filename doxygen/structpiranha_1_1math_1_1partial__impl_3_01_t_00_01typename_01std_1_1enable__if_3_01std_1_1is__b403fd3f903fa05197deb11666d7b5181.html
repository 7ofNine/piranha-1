Specialisation of the piranha::math::partial() functor for piranha::rational_function.

inline

Call operator.

The call operator will first check whether a custom partial derivative for T was registered via piranha::rational_function::register_custom_derivative(). In such a case, the custom derivative function will be used to compute the return value. Otherwise, the output of piranha::rational_function::partial() will be returned.
