Detailed Description

template<typename T>
struct piranha::math::gcd_impl< T, T, detail::poly_gcd_enabler< T > >

Implementation of piranha::math::gcd() for piranha::polynomial.

This specialisation is enabled if T is an instance of piranha::polynomial that supports piranha::polynomial::gcd(). The piranha::polynomial_gcd_algorithm::automatic algorithm will be used for the computation.
