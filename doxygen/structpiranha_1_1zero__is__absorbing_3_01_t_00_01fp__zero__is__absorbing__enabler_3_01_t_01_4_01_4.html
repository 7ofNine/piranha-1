template<typename T>
struct piranha::zero_is_absorbing< T, fp_zero_is_absorbing_enabler< T > >

Specialisation of piranha::zero_is_absorbing for floating-point types.

Note
This specialisation is enabled if T, after the removal of cv/reference qualifiers, is a C++ floating-point type supporting NaN.

In the presence of NaN, a floating-point zero is not the multiplicative absorbing element.

This type traits requires T to satsify piranha::is_multipliable, after the removal of cv/reference qualifiers.
