Detailed Description

template<typename Stream, typename T>
struct piranha::msgpack_pack_impl< Stream, T, msgpack_scalar_enabler< Stream, T > >

Specialisation of piranha::msgpack_pack() for fundamental C++ types supported by msgpack.

Note
This specialisation is enabled if Stream satisfies piranha::is_msgpack_stream and T is one of the following types:
  • char, signed char, or unsigned char,
  • int or unsigned,
  • long or unsigned long,
  • long long or unsigned long long,
  • float or double,
  • bool.

The call operator will use directly the pack() method of the input msgpack packer.
